MB Pre-Workout 200: The sip before you sweat
MuscleBlaze Pre-Workout 200 is a potent Pre-Workout formula that is crafted to let your body prepare itself for intense workouts. In just a single serving of this product, you’ll feel energized and focused enough to perform heavy-duty exercises. This Pre-Workout fuel is packed with 200mg of caffeine to boost focus, mental alertness, and keeps you active so that the risk of injuries is reduced while you are working out. This powerful Pre-Workout aid is enriched with L-arginine and L-citrulline to enhance pump and increase the nutrient-rich blood flow to your nerves. It is available in exhilarating Green Apple Flavor to let you have a refreshing experience before workouts.
 
Benefits of MB Pre-Workout 200
  • 200mg Caffeine: MuscleBlaze Pre-Workout 200 offers 200mg caffeine that helps in boosting your focus so that you can perform better at the gym with a reduced risk of injuries. It also helps in boosting your mental alertness and keeping you active so that you perform with greater strength without feeling tired. It also fuels you with great energy that helps you enjoy relentless workout sessions.
  • 2200mg L-Citrulline: L-citrulline is a naturally occurring amino acid that helps in improving athletic performance. It helps in minimizing the fatigue that is usually the result of a high-intensity workout. It also boosts nitric oxide output that eventually leads to greater muscle growth with time.
  • 1500mg L-Arginine: MuscleBlaze Pre-Workout 200 is packed with 1500mg L-arginine, an amino acid that helps in regulating nitric oxide levels. It is a complex dietary supplement containing alpha-ketoglutarate. It helps in enhancing the levels of nitric oxide in your body to let you have greater pumps.
  • Zero added sugar: It also contains no added sugar and is ideal for all fitness lovers who are looking for that zing of energy right before they sweat it out at the gym. It is available in refreshing Furious Grape flavor.
  • Highly Affordable: MuscleBlaze Pre-Workout 200 offers great value at a very affordable price. Unleash Your true power with this ultimate powerhouse of energy.
 
How to consume?
Add 1 Heaping Scoop (5g) to 300 mL chilled Water and shake for 15 to 30 Seconds in a shaker vigorously until uniform mixing and consume within 20-30 minutes before working out.
 
Recommended 1 Serving a day or as suggested by your dietitian.
